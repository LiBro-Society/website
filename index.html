About LiBro Society

LiBro Society stands for memory, mutual aid, and resistance against erasure. We work with survivors, creators, and everyday witnesses to publish truth, feed those in need, and build a new culture of decentralized support.

Active Campaigns

  • Free Feeding: Mutual aid meals for underserved communities.
  • LiBro Vouchers: Trust-based peer-to-peer value exchange.
  • Witness Protocol: Publishing evidence, letters, and testimonies on Arweave.
