📍 Easy Guide to Starting Your LiBro Society

🎯 Purpose

Start your own LiBro Society easily and quickly, connecting your friends and community through simple activities and trades using LiBro Vouchers (LV).

✅ Simple Steps to Get Started

🌟 Step 1: Be the Local Champion

  • Spread awareness about LiBro in your area (e.g., classmates, social posts).
  • Organize community gatherings or trades.
  • Teach others about LiBro Vouchers.
“I'm starting a LiBro Society here! Let's trade and support each other — message me to join!”

📢 Step 2: Create a Simple Online Group

  • Name it like “LiBro Society – [Your Area/School]”.
  • Post a welcome message, tips, and challenges.
  • Invite friends from school or neighborhood.

🎉 Step 3: Organize Your First Small Event

  • Snack-sharing, book barters, or “Share a Voucher, Share a Story”.
  • Start by giving vouchers to friends to build excitement.

📸 Step 4: Share Stories and Simple Activities

  • Post about trades or successes (e.g., “I traded my LiBro Voucher for snacks today!”)
  • Run weekly missions (e.g., “Tell a new friend about LiBro”).

📈 Step 5: Easy Check-ins and Updates

  • Ask how many vouchers were used, share stories or ideas.
  • Do this through simple chats or DMs.

🌱 Step 6: Grow Your Community Gently

  • Focus on small, local exchanges with classmates or friends.
  • Invite shops or vendors to accept LiBro Vouchers.
  • Offer handwritten “We Accept LiBro” signs.
  • Host raffles, art contests, or community snack events.

📌 Easy Starter Kit Checklist

  • Use LiBro Flyers during gatherings — they include a QR code linking to more info.
  • Keep it fun and simple. Share often. Celebrate every little win!
